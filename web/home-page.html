Hello!  I'm Sarah Harissis.

I'm a Front-End developer with strong background in graphic design and user experience. I currently work for BarkleyREI, a full-service digital agency with clientele primarily in the tourism and higher education industries. My specialty is developing design compositions into beautiful, fully-functional and captivating web experiences. I'm particularly skilled with making ambitious visual concepts come to life with CSS.

I'm known to be an avid problem solver with meticulous attention to detail, which shines through in all of the work I produce. My foundation in design and UX is a rare hybrid of form and function, making me stand out amoungst my colleagues.

About

I grew up in a ski town in northern Vermont. In 2007, I moved to Pittsburgh, PA to attend Carnegie Mellon University, where I earned my B.F.A. in Communication Design and Human-Computer Interaction. After graduation, I began my career in user experience, but quickly discovered I had a knack for web development that I was also eager to explore. Since then, I've been learning and refining my development skillset, while utilizing my background in design and UX to make informed decisions as I code.

I spend most of my free time cooking, being outdoors, and hanging out with Butters, my adorable golden retriever. I also enjoy skiing, singing, survivalism, volunteering, traveling, prog rock, good friends, and bad puns.
